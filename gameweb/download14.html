    About game

    Description:Red Faction is a shooting game. This was developed under the banner of Volition, Inc. It was released in 2001 and was published by THQ.

    Red Faction 1 PC Game is plotted as in the late 21st century on Mars a company Ultor Corporation has started a mining operation. It is because there is a deficit of the natural minerals and fuels in the earth. In the company there is a miner named Parker becomes rebellion after he sees that the company is treating the miners like slaves. From there the player has to take control of Parker and have to fight against the Ultor Corporation in order to get out of Mars.
