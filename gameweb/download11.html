    About game

    As a part of the Hitman game series, Hitman 3 stands as the eighth main installment in the franchise and serves as the conclusive third segment of the World of Assassination trilogy. Similar to its forerunner, Hitman 3 features Agent 47 as the protagonist, and players control him from a third-person perspective. The primary objective of the game revolves around executing assassinations, prompting the agent to embark on a global journey to various locations. Notably, Dubai, Dartmoor, Berlin, and Chongqing make their debut in the Hitman series. The game introduces PlayStation VR compatibility for the PlayStation 4 version, extending to levels imported from the two preceding entries. Unlike Hitman 2, there is an absence of multiplayer and ghost modes in Hitman 3. Developers describe the game’s setting as “more serious, more mature, and darker” compared to its predecessors. Alongside classic game modes, the inclusion of Elusive Targets and Contracts Mode is announced, ensuring a continuous addition of missions even after completing the main storyline.
