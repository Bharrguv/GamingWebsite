    About game

    "Call of Duty" is a popular first-person shooter video game franchise that began in 2003. Initially focused on World War II, the series has since expanded to include modern warfare, futuristic settings, and even historical conflicts. Players typically engage in both single-player campaigns, which feature immersive storylines and character development, and multiplayer modes that emphasize competitive gameplay.

    The franchise is known for its intense action, realistic graphics, and various game modes, including team-based and battle royale formats. It has garnered a large following and has become a cultural phenomenon, with numerous titles and spin-offs released across various gaming platforms. The series also features iconic weapons, customizable loadouts, and a variety of maps inspired by real-world locations.
